These are all the dominions at the moment. You can click on anyone's name to see an overview of their dominion.

Each dominion has a penalty to their defense (DP) It worses by 0.33% per tick and resets to zero when they get invaded. Similarly, they have a bonus to their offense (OP). It improves by 0.33% per tick and resets to zero when they invade someone.

Highest OP tracks the greatest total offense that dominion has sent on an invasion. It doesn't represent their maximum potential, but it's a useful tool for determining if you have enough defense.
