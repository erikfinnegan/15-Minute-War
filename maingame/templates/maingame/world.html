Details

These are all the dominions at the moment. When someone makes an invasion, the units they send take 12 ticks to return home, leaving them more vulnerable to attack in the meantime. The land they conquer also takes 12 ticks to arrive and they can't attack again until it does.

On an invasion, the attacker takes 10% casualties if they win and 15% casualties if they lose. The defender takes 5% casualties if they lose, 2% casualties if they win, and no casualties if the offense less than half the defense. Of course, the attacker will likely win because the total offense and defense are both public information.

Every tick, a dominion gains -0.5% defense. This resets when that dominion gets invaded. Also gain grudges against anyone who invades them, slowly increasing their offense against their previous attackers until they finally retaliate, at which point their grudge against that particular dominion is cleared.
